FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your first booking. Still have questions? We love to chat!

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?▼
Not at all! Many of our clients provide a key or door code so we can clean while they're at work or running errands. We're fully insured and bonded, so you can trust us completely in your home.
What's the difference between Standard and Deep Cleaning?▼
Standard cleaning covers regular maintenance — dusting, vacuuming, mopping, bathrooms, and kitchen surfaces. Deep cleaning goes further: inside the oven and refrigerator, behind appliances, baseboards, window sills, inside cabinets, and more. We recommend a deep clean for first-time clients.
Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?▼
Yes! We bring everything we need including professional-grade, eco-friendly cleaning products and all equipment. If you have a preferred product you'd like us to use, or something to avoid, just let us know in your booking notes.
Do you clean with pets in the home?▼
Absolutely — we love animals! We use pet-safe cleaning products and are experienced working around all kinds of pets. Just let us know about your furry family members when you book so we can be prepared.
How do I pay?▼
We accept cash, check, Venmo, Zelle, and major credit cards. Payment is due on the day of service. For recurring clients, we offer convenient automatic billing options. We'll discuss payment details when we confirm your booking.
What if I'm not happy with the cleaning?▼
Your satisfaction is our #1 priority. If something isn't right, just let us know within 24 hours and we'll come back and make it right — free of charge. We stand behind our work 100%.
How far in advance should I book?▼
We recommend booking at least 3–5 days in advance to secure your preferred date and time. For same-week availability, give us a call directly — we're happy to try to fit you in!
